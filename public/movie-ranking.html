The Best Movies According to Jade

My top 3 movies of all time.

A Knights Tale

A fun movie about a man reaching for the stars. A medieval tale with a modern twist.

The Lord of the Rings

This movie truly illustrates the hero's journey.

It's the perfect example of what friendship, honor, and adventure amongst an unlikely group of people can look like.

Spirited Away

Alice in Wonderland meets exquisite animation. A feast for the eyes.
